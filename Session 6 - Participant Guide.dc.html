Opening Debrief — My Reteach

Reteach I taught (group + access point) Who moved piles — evidence from the new work

The Strength-Based Frame — Notes

"Because you … , your students were able to …"

A named teacher move + a counted student outcome. Example: "Because you had partners rehearse the sentence before the share-out, 16 of 18 students gave a complete sentence — including two of your ELs."

Reflective Question Menu — FS IPG Alignment Guides

  • Who participated in that activity — and who found a way to be present without practicing?
  • Which access point did the most work in this lesson? How do you know?
  • Where did students struggle productively — and where did it stop being productive?
  • If you taught this lesson again tomorrow, which scaffold would you keep, fade, or add?

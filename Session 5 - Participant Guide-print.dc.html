The Sorting Protocol — Notes

  • Mastered — meets the task. Next move: the stretch.
  • Approaching — one error pattern away. Next move: quick reinforce.
  • Needs access point — the lesson didn't reach them yet. Next move: reteach that supplies the missing access point. Not "low group."

Name needs precisely: not "struggles with reading" but "confuses digraphs ch/sh" or "writes labels, not complete sentences." The precise name IS the reteach plan.

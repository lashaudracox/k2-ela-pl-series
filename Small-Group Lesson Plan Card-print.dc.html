Small-Group Lesson Plan Card

Two cards per page — one 15-minute teacher-table group each. Print a stack, keep them on a ring at the table. Built to pair with the Reteach Planning Template and the rotation chart.

Teacher-Table Plan (~15 min)
Group: ______________
Date: __________
Need: ______________________________
Warm up (2 min)
review a secure skill
Access point (3 min)
visual • discourse • model • prior knowledge
Every-voice practice (7 min)
boards, tapping, choral — all students, every round
Quick check (3 min)
the exit task — who moved?
Result: ____ of ____ got it  •  still needs it: ______________________

The shape is the scaffold: 2-3-7-3 keeps the group moving and puts most of the time in student practice, not teacher talk. If the quick check shows no movement after two cards for the same need, bring it to planning — wrong access point, or an RTI² conversation.

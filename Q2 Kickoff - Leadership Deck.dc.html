THE BIG IDEA

The Quarter 2 Shift: From Access to Independence

Scaffolds fade
Q1's supports come down on evidence — planned exits, tracked on the fade log, never dropped cold.
Writing grows
Modeled → shared → independent writing inside the Wonders block — counting who writes, not who watches.
Groups run on need
Small groups re-form from every sort; workstations hold participation with built-in accountability.
Same through-line, higher bar: independence is access that no longer needs us standing next to it.

CONTINUITY

What Stays Exactly the Same

The cadence: biweekly, 60 minutes, inside ELA planning — no new meetings.
The evidence chain: every session opens with last session's classroom evidence and closes with a commitment.
The partners: ESL & SPED co-plan in S9 and S11.
The lens: the same walkthrough checklist — extended to fade evidence and writing participation.
FOUR CONCRETE ASKS

What We Ask of Leadership in Q2

1
Keep protecting the block — the biweekly planning time held through the holiday season.
2
Schedule the partners — ESL and SPED coverage for Sessions 9 and 11, confirmed a week ahead.
3
Extend the walkthrough lens — look for fading scaffolds and writing participation, and keep counting.
4
Protect the winter screening window — the mid-year review needs clean data to set the Quarter 3 focus.

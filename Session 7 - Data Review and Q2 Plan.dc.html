PART 1

Three Data Sources, One Question

DID ACCESS GROW? • PARTICIPANT GUIDE P. 2

Triangulating the Quarter

Participation counts
From walkthroughs and your own tallies. August baseline: 11/18 and 14/18. Where are we now?
Mastery rates
From your sorted class sets. August baseline: 10/18 mastering the writing task. Which piles grew?
Screening & RTI²
Universal screening movement, and Tier II referrals — are fewer driven by missing Tier I access points?
When all three point the same way, we can trust the story. When they disagree, that disagreement is the finding.

End of Week — Read the Pattern

Names appearing 2+ times in the "not yet" column
What those students have in common — activity type, time of day, language demand, seat?
Next move — the routine, access point, or scaffold aimed at them next week
Keep it light: one activity per day, ten seconds of counting. The visible structure does the work — boards up, signals shown, every voice on cue. If counting feels hard, the structure isn't visible enough yet.
Where it goes: bring the week's sheet to ELA planning and the session debriefs — these counts are the participation line in the team data summary and the movement we celebrate in Session 7.

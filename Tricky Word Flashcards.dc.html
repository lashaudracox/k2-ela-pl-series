Tricky Word Flashcards

Heart-the-tricky-part cards: students tap the regular sounds (blue) and learn the tricky part (red) by heart. Print on cardstock, cut apart. K high-frequency words first — add your grade's list on the blank cards.

Kindergarten Start Smart & Unit 1 (I, can, the, a, see, like)

I
all tricky — a word all by itself ♥
can
no tricky part — tap all three sounds
the
tricky part: e ♥ — tap th, learn the e
a
tricky part: says /uh/ in a sentence ♥
see
tricky part: ee ♥ — two e's, one sound
like
tricky part: i_e team ♥ — the e is silent

Common K–2 Tricky Words (said, was, of, to, you, are)

said
tricky part: ai ♥ — says /e/
was
tricky part: a ♥ — says /uh/; s says /z/
of
tricky part: o ♥ — f says /v/ too!
to
tricky part: o ♥ — says /oo/
you
tricky part: ou ♥ — says /oo/
are
all tricky ♥ — learn it by heart

Blank Cards — write this week's words; blue marker for regular sounds, red for the tricky part

The routine: show the card → students tap the blue sounds → teacher hearts the tricky part and says its sound → read the whole word twice → use it in a sentence with a partner. Review a taught word for three days, then move it to the word wall. A word the sort flags (like said) goes back into the deck.

Sentence Starter Cards

Print on cardstock, cut on the dotted lines. Post the set that matches the routine; fade as students carry the language themselves.

Responding to a Partner

Talking About Words & Sounds

The first sound I hear is ______.

Talking About Stories

The character felt ______ when ______.
First ______, then ______, last ______.
I learned that ______.
I wonder ______.
How to use them as scaffolds: model the starter aloud first • have students "say it with your partner first" before the share-out • post only the set in use — four cards, not sixteen • for ELs, pair each card with a quick gesture • fade a card once the sort or your count shows students using the frame on their own.

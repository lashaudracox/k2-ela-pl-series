Q1 Series Overview
K–2 ELA PROFESSIONAL LEARNING • QUARTER 1 • SERIES OVERVIEW

Mastering Foundational Skills for ALL Students

A biweekly professional learning series for K–2 teachers • August–October • Presenter: LaShaudra Cox • Ross Elementary

Why This Series

Our own walkthrough data shows the gap: lessons follow the guides, yet in one K–2 snapshot only 11 of 18 students participated in the warm-up, 14 of 18 in Chaining, and 10 of 18 mastered the writing task — up to 39% never practiced the skill. Fidelity is necessary but not sufficient. Access is planned, not improvised — and this series builds that planning into the work teachers already do.

What Anchors the Work

  • TN Foundational Skills IPG — the Culture of Learning descriptors and Core Action 3: ALL students listening, speaking, reading, writing.
  • TDOE Summer Sessions — intellectual preparation and inclusive strategies for ELs and students with IEPs.
  • MSCS Wonders curriculum maps (2025–26) — Sessions 2–5 annotate real upcoming K, 1, and 2 lessons, not samples.

How every session runs

Open with last session's classroom evidence → new learning → working time in teachers' own guides and student work → an exit-ticket commitment carried back to the classroom.

How we'll measure success

Participation counts trend from 11/18 and 14/18 toward all; mastery grows from the 10/18 baseline; fewer Tier II referrals driven by missing Tier I access points.

The through-line

What we plan together is what we teach, what walkthroughs look for, what feedback names, and what student work measures — one coherent system.

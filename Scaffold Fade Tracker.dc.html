Fading, not dropping: shrink it before you remove it — the anchor chart moves farther from the rug, the sentence starter loses a word, the model becomes a reminder. Each step is a check-in row.
Exit evidence is observable: "8 of 8 in the group wrote a complete sentence without the card, two days in a row" — counts and work samples, the same evidence the sort uses.
If it never comes down: a scaffold still standing after two check-ins with no movement isn't fading — bring it to planning: wrong tool, wrong need, or a candidate for the RTI² conversation.

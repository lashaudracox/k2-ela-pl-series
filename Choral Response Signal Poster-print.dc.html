Choral Response Signal Poster
Our Choral Response Signal
One signal. Every voice. All together.
1
TEACHER ASKS
Listen to the whole question. Hands stay down — everyone answers this one.
2
THINK
Quiet think time. Get the answer ready in your head — lips closed.
3
SIGNAL
Watch for it: the teacher's hand drops — that's our cue.
4
EVERYBODY ANSWERS
All voices together — strong and proud. Your voice matters every time.
Teacher note: think time before the signal is the access point — it lets every student, not just the fastest, arrive with an answer. Listen for who is rehearsing vs. mouthing along; that's your count.

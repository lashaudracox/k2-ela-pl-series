Station menu to fill the blank cells: decodable reading (partner) • word work / sound-spelling • listening & responding • writing station • fluency re-read. Write the station name + this week's task in each cell.

Accountability, not busywork: every station produces something that comes back — a whiteboard photo, a recording sheet, a partner-reading tally. If nothing comes back, participation at that station is invisible.
Teach the rotation like phonics: model the transition, practice it, reinforce it (Smart Start does this in weeks 1–3). The signal, the path, and where finished work goes are all routines on your Session 1 menu.

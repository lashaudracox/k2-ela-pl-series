The Turn & Talk Script
1. Ask
Pose the question. Give 5 seconds of think time — no hands.
2. Cue
"Turn and talk — Partner A first." (Alternate who goes first each time.)
3. Listen in
Kneel by 2–3 pairs — especially your watched students. This is your count.
4. Signal back
Choral signal ends the talk. "Boards flat, eyes on me."
5. Share out
Call on pairs you heard — "Tell us what your partner said."
Why assigned partners: "find a partner" quietly excludes the students who need discourse most. A taped A/B card removes the social task so every student's working memory goes to the language, not the logistics.
Access moves: pair ELs with strong language models, not other silent students • post one sentence starter card next to the question • "say it with your partner first" before any whole-group share.

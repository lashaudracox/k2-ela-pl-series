Our Whiteboard Routine
Every board. Every voice. Every time.
1
LISTEN
Marker down. Eyes on the teacher. Hear the whole word.
2
WRITE
Tap the sounds. Write it big. Your best try counts.
3
SHOW
On the signal — boards up! Hold it still so it can be seen.
4
CHECK & ERASE
Compare with the model. Fix it if you need to. Erase and get ready.
Boards Up!
Our signals — say them, show them, every time
"Write it."
Markers move. Voices off. Boards flat.
"3… 2… 1… Boards up!"
Every board in the air — chin high, facing out.
"Check it."
Look at the model. Thumbs up if it matches — fix it if not.
"Erase and ready."
Clean board. Marker capped. Eyes back on the teacher.
We Take Care of Our Boards
So they're ready every day, for every learner
✓
Cap clicks on the marker when we're not writing.
✓
Write on the board — nowhere else.
✓
Erase with the eraser, corner to corner.
✓
Board, marker, eraser back in the caddy — ready for tomorrow.
Teacher note: model each step, practice as a game in weeks 1–3, reinforce with the "Because you…" language — the routine is the scaffold.
